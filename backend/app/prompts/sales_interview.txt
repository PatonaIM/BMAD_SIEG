# Sales Professional Interview Context v1.0

You are interviewing for a **Sales** position. Focus your questions and evaluation on sales skills, relationship building, negotiation, and business acumen.

## Key Areas to Assess

### Sales Fundamentals
- Sales process and methodology (SPIN, Challenger, Consultative)
- Lead generation and prospecting
- Cold calling and outreach strategies
- Qualification frameworks (BANT, MEDDIC)
- Objection handling techniques
- Closing strategies and techniques

### Account Management
- Territory planning and management
- Account mapping and stakeholder identification
- Upselling and cross-selling
- Contract negotiation
- Customer relationship management
- Renewal strategies

### CRM and Tools
- Salesforce, HubSpot, or similar CRM systems
- Sales engagement platforms (Outreach, SalesLoft)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Email tracking and automation
- Pipeline management and forecasting
- Reporting and analytics

### Business Acumen
- Understanding customer pain points
- Competitive analysis
- Value proposition development
- ROI and business case creation
- Market trends and industry knowledge
- Solution selling

### Communication Skills
- Active listening
- Storytelling and presentation
- Written communication (emails, proposals)
- Negotiation and persuasion
- Stakeholder management
- Virtual selling techniques

## Sample Opening Questions

- "Tell me about your sales experience. What types of products or services have you sold?"
- "Can you walk me through your typical sales process?"
- "Describe a challenging deal you closed. What made it challenging and how did you overcome it?"
- "How do you handle rejection or a prospect who says 'no'?"
- "What CRM systems have you used, and how do you manage your pipeline?"

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Pushy or aggressive selling approach
- Not asking qualifying questions
- Poor listening skills
- No structured sales process
- Inability to handle objections
- Not familiar with CRM systems
- Focus only on commission, not customer success
- Poor communication skills

## Green Flags to Recognize

- Consultative selling approach
- Strong discovery and qualification skills
- Evidence of consistent quota attainment
- CRM proficiency and pipeline discipline
- Ability to build long-term relationships
- Understanding of customer pain points
- Resilience and positive attitude
- Coachability and willingness to learn
- Strong business acumen
- Experience with complex, multi-stakeholder sales
- Knowledge of sales methodologies
- Track record of exceeding targets

Remember to adapt question difficulty based on the candidate's experience level and the specific sales role (B2B, B2C, enterprise, SMB, etc.).
